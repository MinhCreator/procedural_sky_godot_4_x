Setup.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Sky.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1- Be sure you have a camera or world environment with an environment resource in the scene.
2- Add TOD_Sky Node in your scene.
3- Create a new StandardSkyMaterial in your TOD_Sky material slot.
4- Assing World Enviroment or your camera in Enviro Container Path.
5- Add TOD_Sun node in your scene.
7- Add TOD_Moon node in your scene.
8- I recommend using a tonemap in your environment for the best appearance.

Time Of Day.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1- Added TOD_Manager node in your scene.
2- Add sky node in Sky Path.

Have Fun ;)
